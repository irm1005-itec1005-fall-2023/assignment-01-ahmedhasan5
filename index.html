Introduction

Apple Inc. is a global technology giant headquartered in Cupertino, California. Founded in 1976, it is renowned for its innovation and iconic product lineup, spanning consumer electronics, software, and services. Apple has consistently shaped the tech industry and consumer culture with its cutting-edge designs and user-friendly interfaces.
